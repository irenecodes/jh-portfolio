Joey Hua is a UX/UI & Service Designer, who creates vibrant and delightful experiences through data-driven products.

Hello!

I’m Joey, a designer of products and services, with a love for illustrations.

Whether I’m iterating on design wireframes, sketching on paper or facilitating design sessions with folks from other disciplines, I’m thrilled to be a co-creator in bettering the everyday services and products that we interact with.

When I’m not designing or doodling, you’ll find me on a hike or exploring the city.

Background

My journey into design wasn’t always clear, but the moment I tried it out, I knew I found my place in the world.

Prior to becoming a designer, I developed a knack for sales and consulting in various settings, from: clinical/medical, hospitality to digital marketing. My academic background in Sociological research, and an inclination towards the creative heavily contributes to the way in which I work.
